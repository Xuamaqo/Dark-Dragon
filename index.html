Ancient Torment: The Return

Join the mysterious world of Ancient Torment

Explore & Adventour

Sorcerer – Master of Destruction

The Sorcerer is a glass cannon, wielding immense fire, death, and energy magic to obliterate enemies. While fragile, they deal massive burst damage, making them one of the most powerful spellcasters.

  • One of the highest DPS classes with powerful AoE.
  • Requires strategic positioning and mana management.
  • Energy Magic – Arcane beams and shocking lightning.
  • Perfect for players who love pure destructive power.

Master the elements and unleash devastation!

Druid – Guardian of Nature

The Druid is a hybrid spellcaster who heals allies, controls enemies, and shapeshifts into powerful beasts. They thrive in both support and combat, adapting to any situation.

  • Extremely versatile – healer, damage dealer, or tank.
  • Nature’s Wrath – Bind, poison, and blind enemies with vines and insects.
  • Perfect for players who love adaptability and strategy.
  • Control enemies with roots and poisons.

Become one with nature and master the wild!

Archer – Master of Precision & Agility

The Archer is a deadly ranged combatant, striking swiftly and keeping enemies at bay with expert marksmanship and crowd control. Nimble and elusive, they dominate the battlefield from a distance.

  • Precision Shots – High-damage attacks with bows, crossbows, and rifles.
  • Traps & Snares – Root, slow, and cripple enemies to maintain distance.
  • Deadly from a distance, with high mobility.
  • Excellent at kiting and controlling enemy movement.

Hone your aim and strike with deadly accuracy!

Warrior – Unstoppable Force

The Warrior is a master of melee combat, dominating the battlefield with sheer strength and resilience. Whether wielding dual weapons for speed, a massive two-hander for devastation, or a shield for defense, they are the frontline of any fight.

  • Dual Wielding – Swift, relentless attacks with two one-handed weapons.
  • Two-Handed Mastery – Slow but devastating strikes that shatter the ground.
  • Shield Defense – Absorb damage and protect allies with impenetrable defenses.
  • Versatile – damage dealer or tank.

Charge into battle and crush your foes with raw power!
